Company Overview

MKSM Trading & Contracting Co. is a Mechanical, Electrical and Plumbing contracting company in EGYPT specialing in build, construct, supply introduction, install, service, upgrade, maintenance of Electro-Mechanical Systems & Networks, Utilities and Equipment.
Also, we can design & Build Electro-Mechanical systems regarding to our Qualified technician engineers Following highest standards of ethics, business conduct, assuring privacy to our clients, providing quality services at competitive terms and prices and compling with safety, health & environment protection standards.
